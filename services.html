Our Services

At Hope Haven Children's Home, we provide care and support to children by meeting their basic needs, supporting their education, protecting their health, and helping them grow in a safe and loving environment.

Shelter and Basic Care

We provide children with a safe place to live, clothing, hygiene items, supervision, and daily care to ensure they feel protected and supported.

Feeding and Nutrition Program

We offer daily meals and nutrition support to help children grow healthy and strong. Food donations also help us keep this program running.

Education Support

We support children through school enrollment, learning materials, tutoring, mentorship, and guidance so they can build a better future.

Health Care and Counselling Support

We help children access basic medical care, emotional counselling, hygiene education, HIV counselling, and referrals to health facilities when needed.

Adoption and Family Reintegration Support

We support family tracing, reintegration, and adoption guidance through proper child protection and legal procedures.
